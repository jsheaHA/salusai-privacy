Salus AI — Privacy Policy

Effective June 2026  ·  Developed by Julian Shea

Salus AI is a personal health tracking app for iPhone and Apple Watch. Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data the app collects, where it lives, and how it is used.

Data We Collect

  • Health & fitness data read from Apple Health: steps, heart rate, HRV, sleep, workouts, body composition, and more.
  • Manually logged entries: food and nutrition, hydration, GI symptoms, medications and supplements, cold plunge sessions, cycle tracking, and daily check-ins.
  • Imported lab results and ECG data from PDF documents.
  • Personal profile information: biological sex, date of birth, and height — stored locally only.
  • Photos of food and medications, used only during AI analysis requests (see AI Analysis section below).

How Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored on your device and in your personal iCloud private database (CloudKit). Only you can access your iCloud data — Apple cannot read its contents, and the developer has no access to it. No data is stored on external servers belonging to this app.

AI Analysis — Anthropic Claude

When you request AI-powered analysis, a structured summary of your health data is sent to Anthropic's Claude API. Here is exactly how that works:

  • What IS sent: anonymised health metrics, lab values, symptoms, medication names, and other logged data within the time ranges you configure in Profile → AI Integration.
  • What is NEVER sent: your name, date of birth, phone number, or any direct personal identifier.
  • Photos: food and medication photos are sent to Claude's vision API only during active analysis requests. They are processed in the moment and are not retained by Anthropic after the request completes.
  • You control exactly which health topics are shared with the AI via Profile → AI Integration.

Anthropic's data handling is governed by their own privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy.

Apple HealthKit

Salus AI reads health data from Apple Health only with your explicit permission. HealthKit data is used solely to provide in-app health tracking and insights. We do not sell, share, or use HealthKit data for advertising, marketing, or any purpose unrelated to the app's core health-tracking function. This is a strict requirement of Apple's HealthKit guidelines and a commitment we take seriously.

Third-Party Services

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — AI analysis. See above.
  • Apple iCloud / CloudKit — personal data sync across your devices.
  • TelemetryDeck — privacy-first usage analytics. TelemetryDeck collects anonymised, non-personal signals about which app features are used (e.g. "food log opened") to help improve the app. No health data, no personal identifiers, and no device fingerprinting are ever transmitted. See telemetrydeck.com/privacy.

No advertising networks or crash-reporting services are included in this app.

Data You Control

  • You can delete individual entries at any time from within the app.
  • You can export or delete all your data via Profile → Data & Backup.
  • Revoking HealthKit permissions in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health will stop the app from reading new health data.
  • Deleting the app removes all locally stored data. Your iCloud data can be deleted from iCloud.com or via iOS Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage.

Children's Privacy

Salus AI is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can address it.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. We will indicate the effective date of the latest version at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
